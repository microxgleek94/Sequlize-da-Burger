Welcome to Devour da Burger!

Use the text box below to enter your favorite burger, or just make any ol' burger name. Once you're finished, press the "Submit" button to store your burger. Once you have all your burgers stored, click on the "Devour Me!" button to eat the burgers you've eaten. All burgers that have been eaten will go into the "Devoured" bin.
